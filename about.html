About Weichie.com

Digital Agency in Brussels & New York

Weichie is a Brussels-based creative, technology, and e-commerce optimization agency for extraordinary brands. We leverage our vast experience in creativity, technology, and business to help brands stand out in the digital world.

Weichie was founded in 2019, after an amazing web development career in New York City.
Bob worked there for two years and the dream was to start an agency just like that but in Brussels. We kept our New York connections and are slowly growing in Brussels. Covering a broad range of topics including SaaS products, platform or CMS selection, headless solutions, creative and content.
Are you in need of a new website or webshop or simply want to upgrade your current, old website? We’ve got your back!

We work with other founders, freelancers, consultants, and entrepreneurs to help solve business problems and challenges.
