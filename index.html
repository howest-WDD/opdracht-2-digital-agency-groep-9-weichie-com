Weichie is a web design agency based in Brussels. A small, tight-knit team of digital professionals, designers & developers. We strive to improve on a daily basis and go above and beyond for you and your business.

The digital world is rapidly evolving and we make sure your business will come out ahead. There is no middle man here. No project managers or salesman that leave you hanging. You are free to communicate with the people that are actually working on your website. We are all hands-on professionals and will tell you if something is wrong. – and vice versa of course.

We believe that our passion and dedication to our client’s success are clearly shown in the websites we deliver.
